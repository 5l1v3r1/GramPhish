How It Works

Welcome Aboard. Insta Followers is a free to use Instagram Growth and Automation tool. After you key in your Instagram credentials our servers will generate a private token from your account.

Get back to your Instagram account and you may see a warning page stating "Suspicious Login Attempt" (Don't worry It may take some time to show up. Check again later and you will see it). Click on the button that says "This Was Me" as our servers were trying to connect to your account.
If you have followed the above instructions carefully, you have successfully connected your account and may start using our services.
